\v 37 Abinda ka shuka ba jikin da zaya kasance bane, amma kwayar irin ne kawai. Wanda zaya zama alkama ko wani abu daban. \v 38 Amma Allah zaya bashi jiki yadda ya zaba, ga kowane kwayar iri da nashi jikin. \v 39 Ba duka jiki ne yake iri daya ba. A maimako, akwai jiki irin na mutane, akwai kuma wani irin na dabbobi, kuma wani jikin irin na tsuntsaye, kuma da irin na kifi.
