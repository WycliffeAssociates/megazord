\v 41 \v 40 Akwai kuma jikina na samaniya da jikina  na duniya. Amma daukakar jikin samaniya wata daban ce kuma daukakar jikin duniya daban ce. \v41 Akwai daukaka irin ta rana, da kuma wata daukakar irin ta wata, da kuma wata daukakar irin ta taurari. Domin wani tauraron ya bambanta da wani wajen daukaka.
