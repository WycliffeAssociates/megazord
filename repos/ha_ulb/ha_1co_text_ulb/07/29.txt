\v 29 Amma wannan nike fadi ya'nuwa: lokaci ya kure. Daga yanzu, bari wadanda suke da mata suyi rayuwa kamar basu da su. \v 30  Masu kuka su zama kamar marasa kuka, masu farinciki kamar marasa farinciki, masu sayen abubuwa kamar marasa komai. \v 31 Wadanda suke harka da duniya kamar ba su harka da ita, domin ka'idar duniyan nan tana kawowa ga karshe.
