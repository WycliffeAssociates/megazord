\v 36 Amma idan wani yana tunani da cewa baya yin abinda ya dace ga budurwarsa- idan ta wuce shekarun aure, kuma hakan ya zama dole- sai yayi abinda yake so. Ba zunubi yake yi ba. Sai suyi aure. \v 37 Amma idan ya tsaya da karfi a zuciyarsa, idan baya shan wani matsi kuma yana iya kame kansa, har ya kudurta a zuciyarsa yayi haka, wato ya kiyaye budurwarsa da yake tashi, to hakan ya yi daidai. \v 38 Don haka, shi wanda ya auri budurwarsa yayi daidai, sannan shi wanda ya zabi yaki yin aure yafi yin daidai.
