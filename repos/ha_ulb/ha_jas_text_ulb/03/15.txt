\v 15 Wannan hikima ba irin wadda take saukowa daga sama ba ce, a maimakon haka, ta duniya ce, mara ruhaniya, ta aljanu. \v 16 Domin duk inda kishi da kazamin buri suke, a nan ake samun hargitsi da kowanne irin aikin mugunta. \v 17 Amma hikiman nan ta sama, da farko dai tsattsarka ce, mai lumana ce, kamila ce, mai saukin kai, mai tsananin jinkai, mai yawan alheri, mara gajiya, sahihiya kuma. \v 18 Kuma 'ya'yan adalci wanda ake shukawa a cikin salama a tsakanin wadanda ke kulla zumunci.
