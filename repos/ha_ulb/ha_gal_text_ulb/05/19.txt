\v 19 Yanzu ayyukan jiki a bayyane suke. Sune al'amuran lalata, rashin tsarki, sha'awoyi, \v 20 bautar gumaka, sihiri, yawan fada, jayayya, kishi, zafin fushi, gasa, tsattsaguwa, hamayya, \v 21 hassada, buguwa, buguwa da tarzoma, da dai sauran irin wadannan abubuwa. Na gargade ku, kamar yadda a da na gargade ku, cewa wadanda suke aikata wadannan abubuwa ba za su gaji mulkin Allah ba.
