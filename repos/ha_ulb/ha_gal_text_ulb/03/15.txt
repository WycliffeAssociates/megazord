\v 15 'Yan'uwa, ina magana bisa ga ka'idojin mutane. Idan mutane sun yi yarjejeniya, babu mai kawar da shi ko a kara shi. \v 16 Yanzu fa, an fada alkawaran nan ne ga Ibrahim da zuriyarsa. Bai ce, ''ga zuriya ba'' da manufar masu yawa; amma a maimakon haka ga daya kadai, ''ga zuriyar ka,'' wanda shine Almasihu.
