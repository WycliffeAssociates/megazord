\v 14 Ku yi kowanne abu ba tare da gunaguni da gardama ba. \v 15 Domin ku zama marasa abin zargi kuma masu gaskiya, 'ya'yan Allah marasa aibi. Ku yi haka domin ku haskaka kamar haske a cikin wannan duniya, a tsakiyar karkatacciyar da gurbatacciyar tsara. \v 16 Ku rike kalmar rai da karfi domin in sami dalilin daukaka Almasihu a ranarsa. Sa'annan zan san cewa ban yi tseren banza ba, ban kuma yi wahalar banza ba.
