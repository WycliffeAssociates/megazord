\v 3 Idan wasu yahudawa basu bada gaskiya ba fa? Rashin bangaskiyarsu zai hana amincin Allah aiki? \v 4 Bazai taba zama haka ba. Maimakon haka, bari Allah ya zama mai gaskiya, ko da kowanne mutum makaryaci ne.Kamar yadda aka rubuta, "Domin a nuna kai mai adalci cikin maganganunka, kuma kayi nasara a lokacin da aka kawo ka gaban shari'a."
