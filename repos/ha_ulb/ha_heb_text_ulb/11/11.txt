\v 11 Ta dalilin bangaskiya, Ibrahim ya sami haihuwa ko da shike shi da matarsa Saratu sun yi nisa a cikin shekaru, kuma Saratu bakarariya ce, ta kuma wuce lokacin haihuwa. Tun da shike sun amince da wanda ya yi musu alkawari mai aminci ne. \v 12 Sabo da haka, ta wurin mutumin nan daya, wanda ya ke kamar matacce ne, aka haifi zuriya mai dimbin yawa, su ka yawaita kamar taurari a sama kamar yashi a bakin teku, wanda ba shi kidayuwa.
