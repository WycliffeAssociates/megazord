\v 13 13 To idan jinin awaki da na bajimai da yayyafa tokar karsana bisa wadanda suka bata tsarkinsu yana tsarkake su ya wanke jikkunansu. \v 14 To balle kuma jinin Almasihu, wanda ta wurin Ruhu madawwami ya mika kansa hadaya marar aibi ga Allah, wanda yake wanke lamirinmu daga matattun ayyuka domin muyi bautar Allah mai rai? \v 15 Domin wannan dalili, Almasihu shine magamin sabon alkawari. Wannan kuwa ya kasance ne domin anyi mutuwar da ta 'yantar da wadanda ke karkashin tsohon alkawari daga sakamakon zunubansu, saboda wadanda Allah ya kira su karbi alkawarin gadonsu madawwami.
