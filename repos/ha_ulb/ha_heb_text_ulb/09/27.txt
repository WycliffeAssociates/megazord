\v 27 Kamar yadda aka kaddara wa kowa ya mutu sau daya tak, kuma bayan haka sai shari'a. \v 28 Haka kuma Almasihu, wanda aka mika shi sau daya tak domin ya dauke zunuban mutane da yawa, zai kuma bayyana karo na biyu, ba domin yin aikin kawar da zunubi ba, amma domin ceton wadanda suke jiran zuwansa da hakuri.
