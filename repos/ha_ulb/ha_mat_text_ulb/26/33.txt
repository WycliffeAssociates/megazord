\v 33 Amma Bitrus yace masa, "Ko dama duka zasu fadi sabili da kai, ni kam bazan fadi ba." \v 34 Yesu yace masa, ''Hakika ina gaya maka, a wannan daren kafin carar zakara, za ka yi musun sani na sau uku." \v 35 Bitrus yace masa, koda zan mutu tare da kai, bazan yi musunka ba." Sai sauran almajiran suma suka fadi haka.
