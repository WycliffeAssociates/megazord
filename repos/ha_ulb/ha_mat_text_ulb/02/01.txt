\c 2 \v 1 Bayanda  aka haifi Yesu a Baitalami ta kasar Yahudiya, a zamanin sarki Hirudus, sai ga wadansu masana daga gabas suka zo Urushalima, suna cewa, \v 2 ''Ina wanda aka haifa sarkin Yahudawa? Mun ga tauraronsa a gabas mun kuma zo mu yi masa sujada.'' \v 3 Da sarki Hirudus ya ji haka, sai ya damu kwarai, haka kuma dukan mutanen Urushalima.
