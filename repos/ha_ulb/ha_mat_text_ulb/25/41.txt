\v 41 Sa'annan zai cewa wadanda suke hannunsa na hagu, 'Ku rabu da ni, ku la'anannu, ku tafi cikin wutar jahannama da aka shirya wa Shaidan da aljannunsa, \v 42 domin ina jin yunwa baku bani abinci ba; ina jin kishi ba ku bani ruwa ba; \v 43 Ina bakunci amma baku bani masauki ba; ina huntanci, baku tufasar da ni ba; ina ciwo kuma ina kurkuku, baku kula da ni ba.'
