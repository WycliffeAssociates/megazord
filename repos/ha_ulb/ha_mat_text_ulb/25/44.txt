\v 44 Sa'annan suma zasu amsa su ce, 'Ubangiji, yaushe muka ganka kana jin yunwa, ko kishin ruwa, ko kana bakunci, ko kana huntanci, ko ciwo, kuma a kurkuku, da bamu yi maka hidima ba?' \v 45 Sa'annan zai amsa ya ce masu, 'Ina gaya maku hakika abin da baku iya yiwa mafi karanta daga cikin wadannan ba, ba ku yi mani ba.' \v 46 Wadannan kuwa zasu tafi cikin madawwamiyar azaba amma adalai zuwa rai madawwami."
