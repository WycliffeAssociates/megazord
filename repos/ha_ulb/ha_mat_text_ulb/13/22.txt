\v 22 Shi wanda aka shuka a cikin kayayuwa wannan shine wanda ya ji maganar, amma dawainiyar duniya da kuma yaudarar dukiya suka shake maganar, sai ya kasa ba da 'ya'ya. \v 23 Shi wanda aka shuka a kasa mai kyau, wannan shine wanda ya ji maganar, ya kuma fahimce ta. Wannan shine wanda ba da 'ya'ya da gaske; wadansu ribi dari, wadansu sittin, wasu kuma talatin."
