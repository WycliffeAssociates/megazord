\v 25 Daga ina baftismar Yahaya ta fito, daga sama ko kuma daga mutune?" Sai suka yi shawara a tsakanin su, suka ce, "In munce, 'daga sama,' zai ce mana, 'don me bamu gaskanta dashi ba?' \v 26 Amma in munce, ' daga mutune,' muna tsoron jama'a, saboda sun san Yahaya annabi ne." \v 27 Sai suka amsa ma Yesu suka ce, ''Bamu sani ba." Shi ma yace masu, "Nima bazan gaya maku ko da wane iko nake yin abubuwan nan ba.
