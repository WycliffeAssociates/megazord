\v 7 Sai suka ce masa, ''To me yasa Musa ya umarcemu mu bada takardar saki, mu kuma kore ta?'' \v 8 Sai ya ce masu, ''Saboda taurin zuciyarku, shi yasa Musa ya yarda maku ku saki matanku, amma da farko ba haka yake ba. \v 9 Ina gaya maku, duk wanda ya saki matarsa in ba saboda zina ba, ya kuma auri wata, yana zina kenan. Wanda kuma ya auri macen da aka saka, yana aikata zina.''
