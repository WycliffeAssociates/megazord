\v 15 Ba mu yi fahariya fiye da kima game da aikin wasu ba. Maimakon haka, muna fatan bangaskiyar ku ta karu domin bangaren aikin mu ya kara fadada kwarai, kuma a daidai iyakarsa. \v 16 Muna fatan haka, domin mu kai bishara zuwa yankunan da ke gaba da naku. Ba za mu yi fahariya akan aikin da aka yi a yankin wani ba.
