\v 5 Ku kashe sha'awacce-sha'awaccen da suke a duniya - zina da fasikanci, rashin tsarki, muguwar sha'awa, mummunar buri, da kuma kwadayi, wadda shine bautar gumaka. \v 6 Saboda wandannan abubuwa ne fushin Allah ke zuwa a kan marasa biyayya. \v 7 A cikin wadannan abubuwa ne kuka yi rayuwa a da da kuke zauna a cikinsu. \v 8 Amma yanzu dole ku kawar da wadannan - zafin rai, fushi, mugayen manufofi, zage-zage, da kazamar magana daga bakinku.
