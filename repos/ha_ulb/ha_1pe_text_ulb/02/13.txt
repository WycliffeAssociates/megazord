\v 13 Kuyi biyayya ga kowacce hukuma ta mutane sabili da Ubangiji, ko ga sarki domin shi ne shugaba. \v 14 Ko kuwa ga masu mulki aikakkunsa ne domin su hori masu aikata mugunta da yabawa masu aikata nagarta. \v 15 Gama wannan nufin Allah ne, ta wurin aikin kirki ku, kwabi jahilcin mutane marassa hikima. \v 16 Kamar 'yan'tattu kada ku mori 'yan'cinku kamar mayafin mugunta, amma ku zama kamar bayin Allah. \v 17 Ku girmama dukkan mutane. Ku kaunaci 'yan'uwa, kuji tsoron Allah. Ku girmama sarki.
