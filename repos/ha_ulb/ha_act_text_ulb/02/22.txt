\v 22 Mutanen Isra'ila, ku saurari wadannan magana. Yesu Banazare, mutumin da Allah ya tabbatar da shi gareku ta wurin manyan ayyuka da ban mamaki da alamu da Allah ya yi ta wurinsa a tsakiyarku, kamar yadda ku da kanku kuka sani. \v 23 Saboda tabbataccen shiri da riga sani na Allah, aka bayar dashi, ku kuma, ta hannun mutane 'yan tawaye, kuka gicciye shi kuma kuka kashe shi. \v 24 Wanda Allah ya tayar, bayan ya cire zafin mutuwa daga gareshi, saboda ba zai yiwu ba ta rike shi.
