\v 12 Da shike tashar ba za ta yi dadin zama da hunturu ba, mafi yawa sun ba da shawara a bar wurin, in maiyiwuwa ne muga mun kai birnin Finikiya, don mu yi hunturu a can. Finikiya tashar jirgin ruwa ce  a  Karita, tana fuskantar  arewa maso gabas da  kudu maso gabas. \v 13 Sa'adda iska daga kudu ta huro a hankali, sun tsammaci bukatarsu ta biya. Sai suka janye linzamin jirgin ruwan sukabi ta Karita kusa da gaba.
