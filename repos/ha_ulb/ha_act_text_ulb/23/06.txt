\v 6 Da Bulus ya ga cewa, daya bangaren Sadukiyawa ne dayan kuma Farisawa, ya yi magana da karfi a majalisa, '''Yan'uwa, ni Bafarise ne, dan Farisawa. Don tabbacin tashin matattu ne kuna shari'anta ni.'' \v 7 Daya fadi haka, mahawara ta fara a tsakanin Farisawa da Sadukiyawa, sai taron ya rabu kashi biyu. \v 8 Don Sadukiyawa suka ce babu tashin matattu, babu mala'iku, babu kuma ruhohi, amma Farisawa suka ce dukan abubuwan nan sun kasance.
