\c 24 \v 1 Bayan kwanaki biyar, sai Ananiyas babban firist,  Wasu dattawa da wani masanin shari'a mai suna Tartilus, sun tafi can. Suka kai karar Bulus gaban gwamna. \v 2 Lokacin da Bulus ya tsaya gaban gwamna, Tartilus ya fara zarginsa yace wa gwamnan, "Saboda kai mun sami zaman lafiya; sa'annan hangen gabanka ya kawo gyara a kasarmu; \v 3 don haka duk abin da ka yi mun karba da godiya, ya mai girma Filikus.
