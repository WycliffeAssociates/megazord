\v 3 Idan ya kasance wani na koyar da wani abu dabam bai kuma yarda da amintaciyar koyarwa ba, wato maganar Ubangijinmu Yesu Almasihu. Alal misali ba su amince da koyarwa da ke kai ga Allahntaka ba. \v 4 Wannan mutum mai girman kai ne, kuma bai san komai ba. A maimakon haka, yana da matukar jayayya da gardama game da kalmomi. Wadannan kalmomi na kawo kishi, da husuma, da zage-zage, da miyagun zace-zace, da \v 5 yawan rashin jituwa tsakanin mutane masu rubabbun hankula. Sun bijire wa gaskiya. Suna tunani cewa ibada hanya ce ta yin arziki." Ka zame daga wadannan abubuwa.
