\v 3 Lallai ne mu godewa Allah kulluyaumin domin ku, yan'uwa. Domin hakan dai dai ne, saboda bangaskiyarku ta karu sosai, kuma kaunar junanku tana karuwa. \v 4 Mu da kanmu muna fahariya da ku a ikilisiyoyin Allah. Muna maganar hakurinku da bangaskiyarku a cikin dukkan tsananin da kuke fuskanta. Muna maganar kuncin da kuke jimrewa. \v 5 Wannan itace alamar shari'ar Allah ta adalci. Sakamakon haka shine za a dauke ku cancantattu na mulkin Allah wanda kuke wahala dominsa.
