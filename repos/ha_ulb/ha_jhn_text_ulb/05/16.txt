\v 16 To saboda haka Yahudawa suka fara tsanantawa Yesu, don ya yi wadannan abubuwa ran Asabar. \v 17 Yesu kuwa ya amsa masu ya ce, "Ubana yana aiki har yanzu, ni ma ina aiki." \v 18 Saboda haka, Yahudawa suka kara nema su kashe shi ba don ya keta Asabar kadai ba, amma kuma domin ya ce Allah Ubansa ne, wato yana maida kansa dai-dai da Allah.
