\v 9 Shugaban bikin ya dandana ruwan da ya zama ruwan inabin, amma bai san inda ya fito ba (amma ma'aikatan wadanda suka jawo ruwan sun sani). Sai ya kira ango \v 10 yace masa, "Kowane mutum yakan raba ruwan inabi mai kyau da farko, kafin mai arhar bayan an shanye mai kyaun. Amma kai ka ajiye mai kyaun sai yanzu."
