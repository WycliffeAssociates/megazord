\v 25 To Saminu Bitrus yana tsaye yana jin dumin.
 Mutanen kuwa suka ce masa, ''Anya kai ma ba cikin almajiransa ka ke ba?'' Sai yayi musu yace, ''A'a, ba na ciki.'' \v 26 Sai wani daga cikin bayin babban firist, wato dan'uwan wanda Bitrus ya fillewa kunne, yace, ''Ashe, ban gan ka tare da shi a lambun ba? \v 27 Bitrus ya sake yin musu, nan da nan kuwa zakara yayi cara.
