\v 19 babban firist ya tambayi Yesu game da almajiransa da koyarwarsa kuma. \v 20 Yesu ya amsa masa, Ai, na yi wa duniya magana a bayyane. Na kuma sha koyarwa a majami'u da Haikali in da Yahudawa duka sukan taru. Ban fadi komai a boye ba. \v 21 Don me kake tambaya ta? Ka tambayi wadanda suka saurare ni a kan abinda na fada, ai, sun san abinda na fada.
