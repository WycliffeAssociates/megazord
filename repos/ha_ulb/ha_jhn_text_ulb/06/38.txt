\v 38 Gama na sauko daga sama, ba domin in bi nufin kaina ba, sai dai nufin wanda ya aiko ni. \v 39 Wannan shi ne nufin wanda ya aiko ni, kada in rasa ko daya daga cikin wadannan da ya bani, sai dai in tashe su a ranar karshe. \v 40 Gama wannan shi ne nufin Ubana, duk wanda yake ganin Dan ya kuma gaskata da shi zai sami rai madawwami, ni kuma zan tashe shi a ranar karshe.
