\v 23 Bayan da sojojin suka gicciye Yesu, sai suka yayyaga rigarsa kashi hudu, kowane soja da nasa, kashin, sai kuma alkyabbar. Alkyabbar kuwa bata da dinki an saka tane daga sama har kasa. \v 24 Sai sukace wa junansu, "Kada mu yaga alkyabbar, amma kuzo mu jefa kuri'a a kan ta mu gani ko wanene zai dauke ta, anyi haka ne domin a cika nassi cewa "Sun raba rigata  a tsakaninsu, sun kuwa jefa kuri'a domin alkyabbata.
