\v 4 Sai Bilatus ya sake fita waje yace masu, "Dubi, zan kawo maku mutumin domin ku sani ban same shi da wani laifi ba", \v 5 Yesu kuwa ya fito, da rawanin kaya da kaya irin na sarauta. Sai Bilatus yace "Ga mutumin nan". \v 6 Da manyan firistocin tare da yan majalisar suka ga Yesu, sai suka yi ta ihu suna cewa "A gicciye shi, A gicciye shi" Sai Bilatus yace masu, "ku dauke shi ku gicciye shi, ni kam ban same shi da wani laifi ba".
