\v 25 Amma na gaya maku gaskiya akwai gwauraye da yawa cikin Isra'ila a zamanin Iliya, lokacin da an rufe sammai shekaru uku da rabi babu ruwa, lokacin da anyi gagarumar yunwa a duk fadin kasar. \v 26 Amma ba a aiki Iliya zuwa wurin waninsu ba, sai wurin gwauruwa da ke a Zarifat can kusa da Sidon. \v 27 Akwai kutare da yawa kuma a Isa'ila a zamanin anabi Elisha, amma babu wanda aka warkar sai dai Na'aman mutumin Suriya kadai.
